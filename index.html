Key Idea

Image editing ‚â° Bridge modeling problem

We formulate image editing as a reverse-time stochastic bridge problem: constructing a bridge from the source distribution $$ p(x_T) $$ to a target distribution $$ p(x_0) \cdot p_{\mathcal{Y}}(y \mid x_0), $$ where \( p_{\mathcal{Y}}(y \mid x_0) \) represents the editing objective.

This formulation provides a principled, theoretical foundation and ensures that edited samples are drawn directly from the desired distribution‚Äîyielding high editing fidelity and faithfulness.

To incorporate multiple editing objectives (e.g., text, style, reference images), we simply extend the target distribution as: $$ p(x_0) \cdot p_{\mathcal{Y}}(y_1 \mid x_0) \cdot p_{\mathcal{Y}}(y_2 \mid x_0) \cdots p_{\mathcal{Y}}(y_n \mid x_0), $$ allowing multiple editing types to be handled simultaneously in a unified framework.
